WHY US

At LipSpeak, we prioritize accessibility, innovation, and user empowerment. Our platform offers a unique, structured, and adaptive approach to mastering lip-reading skills. With real-world video exercises, multi-level training, and real-time feedback, we ensure a personalized experience that boosts confidence and communication abilities for the hearing impaired. Choose LipSpeak—because effective communication is for everyone.

ABOUT US

At LipSpeak, we are dedicated to breaking communication barriers for individuals with hearing impairments. Our mission is to empower users with the skills they need to confidently navigate everyday interactions through innovative lip-reading technology. By combining advanced video analysis with a user-friendly interface, we offer an engaging, personalized, and accessible learning experience. Our team is passionate about creating solutions that make a difference, leveraging cutting-edge technologies like OpenCV and intuitive designs in React Native to ensure a seamless experience. LipSpeak is more than just an app—it's a bridge to a world of better understanding and connection.
